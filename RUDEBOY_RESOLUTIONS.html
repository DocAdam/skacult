Rudeboy Resolutions:

  • Dress sharply but think sharper: Challenge stereotypes and prejudice.
  • Move to the rhythm, but also move against injustice.
  • Keep the faith in the face of adversity.
  • Speak out for those without a voice.
  • Skank with pride, stand with dignity.
  • Remember the roots, but water the future.
  • Build bridges, not walls, with the beats.
  • Respect all, but bow to none unjustly.
  • Carry the message of Ska — unity and joy — everywhere.
  • Never forget: The battle against inequality is fought on the dancefloor and beyond.
